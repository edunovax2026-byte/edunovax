1. Introduction

Welcome to EduNovaX. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. If you have any questions or concerns about our policy, or our practices with regards to your personal information, please contact us.

When you visit our website and use our services, you trust us with your personal information. We take your privacy very seriously. In this privacy policy, we seek to explain to you in the clearest way possible what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have in relation to it.

📊

2. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when registering on the EduNovaX platform. The personal information that we collect depends on the context of your interactions with us.

  • Personal Details: Name, Email Address, and Passwords.
  • Device & Usage Data: IP address, browser type, operating system (OS), and device information collected automatically for security and anti-spam measures.
  • Academic Info: Subjects and semesters you choose to access study materials.
⚙️

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect or receive to provide, operate, and maintain our platform. Specifically, we use your data to:

  • Facilitate account creation and logon process.
  • Send you password reset emails and administrative information.
  • Monitor and prevent fraudulent activities or unauthorized access (using IP & Device logs).
  • Deliver premium educational notes and personalized content to you.
🔒

4. Data Security & Encryption

We use state-of-the-art Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore to store your data. Your passwords are cryptographically hashed and never stored in plain text. We have implemented appropriate technical and organizational security measures designed to protect the security of any personal information we process.

However, please also remember that we cannot guarantee that the internet itself is 100% secure. Although we will do our best to protect your personal information, transmission of personal information to and from our platform is at your own risk.

🍪

5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies (like web beacons and pixels) to access or store information. Cookies help us remember your login sessions so you don't have to log in repeatedly. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, but doing so may affect your ability to use certain features of EduNovaX.
